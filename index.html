Science Lessons for the 2nd Quarter

Momentum



The product of the mass of an object and its velocity.

Describes an object's "Quantity of Motion".

Valence Electron



It is the electrons found on the outermost shell.

The group number tells the number of valence electrons.

Atoms need eight valence electrons to be stable>

Force


The push or pull of an object that causes change in position.

It may change the object's state of motion, direction, size, and shape.
